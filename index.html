Carb timing (your key lever)
Pre-workout (45–60 min before)
30–50g fast carbs. Banana + whey is perfect — primes glycogen without bloat.
Post-workout (within 30 min)
50–80g carbs + 35–40g protein. Rice/oats + whey or Greek yogurt + fruit. Don't skip this window.
Rest of day
Lean protein + veg + moderate fat. Keep carbs low — no workout to burn them.
Plateau prevention
Early warning signs
Energy 2/5 for 2+ days. Sleep under 6 hrs for 3+ nights. Squat stalls 2 consecutive sessions.
Response protocol
Don't deload immediately. Add 200–300 kcal (mainly carbs) on next two training days and retry.
Running interference
Schedule long runs 48+ hrs before your hardest lift day. Tired legs kill squat depth.
HM plan (12 weeks)
Weeks 1–4 · Base
3 runs/week — 1 easy 5k, 1 fartlek/tempo, 1 long run (8–12 km). Keep long run conversational pace.
Weeks 5–8 · Build
Long run to 14–16 km. Add 400m/800m splits for pace. Watch for fatigue overlap with squat days.
Weeks 9–11 · Peak
Long run 18–20 km. Reduce SL5x5 to 2x/week. Increase carbs on long run days.
Week 12 · Taper
Short runs only. Rest, eat well, trust the process.
